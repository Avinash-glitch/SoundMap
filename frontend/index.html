Before you connect Spotify

Have you been added by the owner to this Spotify app?

If not, ask the owner to add your Spotify account email to the app's users. Or create your own Spotify app, add this callback URL, and paste your Client ID and Client Secret below.

https://web-production-7b33.up.railway.app/auth/callback

Yes, the same SoundMap app can be accessed with another Spotify account, but in Spotify development mode that account must be added to the app first.

Your playlist map data is stored in our Supabase database so friend comparison can work. You can choose a private mind map instead, and you can delete your stored data from the map page.
